ABOUT ME

I make art!

I'm a free lance artist and novice streamer. As a hobby I enjoy creating with different mediums of art and streaming my Overwatch competitive matches on Twitch. I've made and sold several paintings, all of which are in particular cartoon style. I also create wooden signs, which are all hand-written in fonts I have designed myself. Some of my art has been featured on social media pages, and can even be found in local shops. I stick mainly to 16x20 canvas' and acrylic paints. On ocassion I will even dabble lightly into Photography. I was fortunate enough to be able to shoot for a professional race team. I'm also on an eSports team for my college. We compete in competitive Overwatch matches. Gaming is a big deal, as it is a dream to become a professional streamer/gamer. Below is a gallery of some of my personal bests and works I'm incredibly proud of accross all mediums I've attempted. For more information please contact me.
